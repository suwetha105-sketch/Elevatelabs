Flipkart Sales Performance Dashboard:

An interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing Flipkart's sales, profit, and order data across product categories, countries, and time. The dashboard combines high-level KPIs with drill-down visuals to support quick, data-driven decision-making for sales and inventory strategy.

Overview:

The dashboard provides a 360° view of business performance, enabling users to filter by product category (Furniture, Office Supplies, Technology) and country (Austria, Belgium, Denmark, Finland, France, and others), and explore trends across customer segments, sub-categories, shipping modes, and time periods.

Key Metrics (KPI Cards):

 Discount              (885.55)
 Total Quantity Sold   (30K)
 Total Profit          (283K)
 Total Sales           (2M)

Dashboard Components:

Sales & Profit by Sub-Category — Horizontal bar chart comparing sales and profit across sub-categories (Art, Binders, Copiers, Phones, Storage, Bookcases, etc.), highlighting top and underperforming product lines.
Profit vs. Sales by Product & Segment — Scatter plot mapping individual products by profit and sales, color-coded by customer segment (Consumer, Corporate, Home Office), useful for spotting high-value outliers and loss-making items.
Profit & Sales by Category and Segment — Area chart breaking down performance across Furniture, Office Supplies, and Technology, segmented by customer type.
Sales & Profit Trend by Year and Month — Dual-axis line chart tracking monthly sales and profit from 2011–2014, revealing seasonality and growth patterns.
Quantity by Segment — Pie chart showing order volume share across Consumer (51.78%), Corporate (31.29%), and Home Office (16.93%) segments.
Sales by Ship Mode — Donut chart illustrating shipping method distribution, with Economy mode accounting for the majority (60.16%) of sales.
Detailed Data Table — Country-level breakdown of profit, sales, quantity, sub-category, category, and region for granular analysis.


Key Insights:

The Consumer segment drives the largest share of order volume, followed by Corporate and Home Office.
Economy shipping is the dominant fulfillment method, suggesting a price-sensitive customer base.
Office Supplies sub-categories (Binders, Art, Copiers) generate consistent sales and profit contributions.
Monthly trends show recurring seasonal peaks, useful for forecasting and inventory planning.


Tools & Technologies:

Power BI Desktop — Data modeling, DAX measures, and visualization
Data Source — Flipkart sales dataset (orders, products, customers, regions)
